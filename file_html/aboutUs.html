Our History

1974

JCC (Jakarta Convention Center) is an iconic conference venue situated in the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. Established in 1974, JCC has played a significant role in hosting numerous prestigious national and international events, making it a cornerstone of Jakarta's convention scene. Throughout its illustrious history, JCC has witnessed groundbreaking conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and captivating musical concerts. Its grand halls have echoed with the voices of world leaders, intellectuals, and visionaries, fostering a spirit of collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Word quickly spread about the exceptional service and atmosphere at JCC, leading to its rapid growth in popularity. With its strategic location, modern amenities, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, JCC became a sought-after destination for conferences, seminars, workshops, and corporate events.

1977

In 1976, JCC made history by hosting the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference. This momentous gathering brought together leaders from diverse nations, fostering dialogue and cooperation on matters of global significance. JCC's state-of-the-art facilities and impeccable management contributed to the success of this landmark event, solidifying its reputation as a premier conference center.

JCC remained at the forefront of innovation, constantly incorporating the latest technological advancements into its infrastructure. The center embraced state-of-the-art audiovisual systems, ensuring seamless presentations and immersive experiences for conference attendees. Equipped with advanced lighting and sound equipment, JCC's event spaces became known for their exceptional atmosphere and functionality.

Certification

Best Venue 2022

The Best Venue 2022 certification is a prestigious recognition awarded to venues that have demonstrated exceptional excellence and innovation in event hosting and management. This certification acknowledges the commitment to providing outstanding services, state-of-the-art facilities, and an unparalleled event experience.

Conference Planning Specialist

The Conference Planning Specialist (CPS) certification is designed for professionals involved in planning and coordinating conferences. This certification demonstrates expertise in selecting venues, managing accommodations, coordinating transportation, and creating engaging conference experiences. CPS holders possess the skills to execute flawless conferences that meet the objectives of attendees and organizers.

Techincal Event Production

The Technical Event Production (TEP) certification is awarded to individuals proficient in technical aspects of event planning and production. TEP holders are skilled in audiovisual setups, lighting design, stage management, and technical troubleshooting. This certification acknowledges the ability to create immersive event experiences through seamless technical execution.
